FAQ

Questions clients ask before booking.

Do I need prior DeFi experience to book a session?
+

No. Sessions are tailored to your current level. Whether you are completely new to DeFi or already managing positions across multiple protocols, the session is structured around where you are and where you want to go.

What exactly happens during a session?
+

Each session is a focused 60-minute 1:1 video call. We work through your specific situation — whether that is building a strategy, evaluating a protocol, or setting up your execution process. Within 24 hours you receive a written plan with clear next steps.

Is this financial advice?
+

No. All consulting is educational and informational in nature. We help you understand DeFi mechanics, evaluate protocols, and build your own risk frameworks — but final decisions are always yours. We do not manage funds or provide regulated financial advice.

How do I pay? Can I pay in crypto?
+

Yes. We accept credit/debit cards via Stripe, ACH bank transfer, PayPal, ATH Móvil, and crypto including USDC, ETH, BTC, and ADA. Payment instructions are sent after booking confirmation.

Can I reschedule or cancel?
+

Yes. Free reschedule up to 24 hours before your session. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. No-shows forfeit the session fee.

Are sessions available in Spanish?
+

Yes. All sessions can be conducted fully in Spanish or English. Written deliverables are also available in your preferred language.
